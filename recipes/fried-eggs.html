Fried eggs

Desription:

Fried eggs every day for a hundred days all days.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • Butter
  • Optional: sausages or meat

Steps:

  1. Put the pan on medium high heat and wait till it is hot.
  2. Give it some butter.
  3. Crush eggs over the pan and let them run down in it. Watch them turn solid.
  4. Put the fried eggs on a plate when you like them.
