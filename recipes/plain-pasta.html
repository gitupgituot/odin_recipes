Plain pasta

Description:

Pasta is the best

Ingredients:

  • The pasta you like 100g
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Water 2l
  • Pot

Steps:

  1. Put the water in the pot.
  2. Put the pot on high heat.
  3. When the water boils drop the pasta into it. And stir it.
  4. Whait 10 minutes and catch one or two pasta bits and taste it. Wait more if neede and taste again. When it is done turn the pot over the strainer.
  5. Add butter and salt.
